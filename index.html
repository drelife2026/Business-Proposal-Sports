02 — Secondary Market

The secondary market
in sports is already here.

The secondary market in sports is growing structurally: fans buy, resell, trade, and search for exclusive, limited-edition, or discontinued pieces.

In jerseys, scarves, and official merchandise, emotion, nostalgia, and club identity increase value over time.

Outside the club
  • Revenue from these transactions does not return to the club
  • The club loses visibility into real market prices
  • Brand experience control is diluted
  • Key insight into fan behavior is not captured
The opportunity already exists: transactions, engagement, and data currently happen outside the club ecosystem.
